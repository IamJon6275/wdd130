Foreward

There are many different environments on this planet, each one with its own wonder and its own peril. Where you will be going is what is going to govern what you need to take with you and how you handle yourself. Whether it is the top of Mount Everest or the middle of the Arabian peninsula. You need to make sure that you have an accurate knowledge of what you will be dealing with on your adventures. This world is not kind to the foolish, you will need to keep your wits about you to avoid dangers from both deadly creatures and the elements if you intend to come back in one piece.

Mountains

Possibly the most difficult environment if just due to the altitude and temperature dangers. Mountains are fairly uniform in terms of temperature and wildlife. Mountains tend to be very cold in the day and even colder at night and very dry regardless of where they are found in the world. Be warned, you could be climbing for most of the trip, if not all of it so proceed with caution when attempting to adventure in this environment. You will not need to worry about fauna as much in the mountains, but there are dangerous animals that like to make this are their home like big cats, wolves and bears.

Rivers

Rivers are quite an easy trip depending on where you are if you do not mind portaging your things every couple of miles. This is a varied environment because it could be located in any longitude, rivers can be found in the heart of South America and the Northernmost reaches of Alaska. Rivers can carry many hidden dangers however, to waterborne parasites to dangerous animals that are looking for a drink or even lurking just underneath the surface.
