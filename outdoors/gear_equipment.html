Foreward

A big part of survival is having the right tool for the right job. Life or death while stranded could hinge on if you brought a flare gun along on your trip. It is particularly important to pack appropriately if you want to make it back home safely and swiftly.

Bags

Bags are essential to your outdoor experience; it will be the thing that is carrying all of your gear and it will also be protecting it from the elements. There is nothing worse than putting on semi-wet undergarments because your cheap, Walmart-bought rope bag got soaked in the rain because you did not want to buy a hiking backpack. A large bag is not always necessary, it depends on the length of your trip and how much gear and food you need to bring with you.

Tents

Much like how a backpack will be keeping your gear safe from the elements, your tent will be doing that for you as you turn in for the night. A tent is a must when adventuring outdoors anywhere that there are mosquitos and biting flies, the last thing you want is a night out in a marsh without something keeping the bugs from literally eating you alive.
